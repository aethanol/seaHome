Our Mission

Our mission is to educate and enable people to affect positive change by involving the voices of people most affected in the system of homelessness.

How it Works

Online Learning

We provide online learning resources about the issues surrounding homelessness and teaching on how to take action in your local community.

Outreach Seminars

Seahome also acts as a physical program for people experiencing homelessness that seeks to educate people on how to get help, and more about the issues surrounding homelessness. We hope to enable a veritable cycle by involving people experiencing homelessness as educators on our online program.
